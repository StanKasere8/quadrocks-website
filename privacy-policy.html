Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 July 2026

Quadrocks Contracting ("Quadrocks," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used, in line with Zimbabwe's Cyber and Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07].

1. Who We Are

Quadrocks Contracting is a construction, structural engineering, architectural design, and geotechnical engineering company operating in Zimbabwe. For any questions about this policy or your personal information, you can reach us at info@quadrocks.co.zw.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number when you submit our contact/quote request form
  • Project details you share, such as service needed, project location, estimated budget, and message content
  • Your email address if you subscribe to our newsletter

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as national ID numbers or financial account details) through this website, and we ask that you avoid including such information in form submissions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to quote requests and enquiries
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send occasional company news or updates, only if you've subscribed to our newsletter
  • Improve our services and this website

We process this information on the basis of your consent, given when you voluntarily submit a form on this site.

4. Third-Party Services

This website uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Formspree — processes and delivers contact form submissions to our email inbox. Formspree may temporarily store submitted data as part of delivering it to us. See Formspree's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Maps — embedded on our Contact page to show our location. Google may collect standard technical data (such as IP address) when this map loads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain business records, after which it is deleted or anonymized unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under Zimbabwe's Cyber and Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to how your information is processed, including for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this won't affect processing that already took place based on your prior consent)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@quadrocks.co.zw. Zimbabwe's data protection authority, POTRAZ (Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe), oversees compliance with the Act.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business use by adults seeking construction and engineering services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Cookies

This website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies of its own. Third-party embeds (such as Google Maps) may set their own cookies when loaded — please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at info@quadrocks.co.zw.

This policy is provided as a general template and does not constitute legal advice. Quadrocks Contracting is encouraged to have this document reviewed by a Zimbabwean legal professional to confirm full compliance with the Cyber and Data Protection Act [Chapter 12:07] and any applicable regulations before relying on it.
